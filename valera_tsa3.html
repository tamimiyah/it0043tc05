Announcement

Attention iTamaraws!

In light of the high heat index this summer, there will be adjustments to the dress code policy for the 3rd Term SY: 2023-2024 to ensure comfort and conducive learning environment for every student.

The following are the adjustments to the dress code policy starting April 25, 2024 until May 30, 2024 :

  • Shorts and skirts are allowed to be worn inside the campus and buildings, provided they must be 1-2 inches above the knee.
  • For footwear, both Crocs and clogs worn with socks are now also allowed.
  • As for tops, the usual dress code policy remains unchanged. Please wear recognized FEU-Tech shirts and uniforms on Mondays and Thursdays, and any appropriate civilian shirt on Wednesdays and Saturdays.

Please be guided accordingly. Thank you.
